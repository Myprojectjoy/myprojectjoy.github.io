Sipra Hazra

With over 35 years of experience in the field of Indian classical music, Sipra Hazra is a dedicated and accomplished music educator. Her expertise spans a wide range of genres including Hindustani Classical, Rabindra Sangeet, and Nazrul Geeti. Known for her patient teaching style and deep knowledge of musical theory, she has trained numerous students across all age groups, helping them not only to master technique but also to understand the soul of music.
